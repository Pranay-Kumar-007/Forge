What is forge actually used for?
Let's start from the developer's perspective. A team is building an application and is midway through the project.
They are assigned few tasks to themselves. How do you handle each and every tasks. How do you sync up properly even if the team is not available.
What if there is an assistant that tells you what to do next. It also tells the team about the development stage and also what all things required to do the change.
This is handled by forge. A platform for developers that is used to solve real life developer problems.
